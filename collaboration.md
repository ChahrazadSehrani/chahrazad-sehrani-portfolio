# Collaboration

I am open to research and engineering collaborations in areas related to:

- Ethical AI
- Digital Twins
- LLM-based reasoning
- Prompt engineering
- Semantic AI
- Hybrid AI
- Human–robot collaboration
- Smart manufacturing
- Explainable AI
- Trustworthy autonomous systems

## Possible collaboration topics

### 1. Ethical reasoning in Digital Twins
Designing LLM-based decision-making pipelines that can reason about trade-offs between productivity, safety, fatigue, fairness, and quality.

### 2. Semantic AI for industrial systems
Representing industrial states using structured formats such as JSON-LD, ontologies, and knowledge graphs.

### 3. Prompt engineering for high-stakes contexts
Designing and evaluating prompts for ethical decision-making in complex scenarios.

### 4. Evaluation of LLM reasoning
Developing qualitative and quantitative protocols to assess reasoning consistency, context sensitivity, robustness, and explainability.

### 5. Human-centered smart manufacturing
Building decision-support systems that protect humans while maintaining industrial performance.
